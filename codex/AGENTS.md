# Codex Global Instructions

## Session Startup — Mandatory

At the beginning of every new Codex session, before starting the user's task,
read `~/Assistants/shared/MESSAGEBOARD.md`. Treat it as the actionable
cross-machine and cross-assistant inbox: note relevant information and act on
unprocessed Codex-directed items before proceeding. Follow the board's own
header and `~/Assistants/shared/SKILL-INFRASTRUCTURE.md` for posting and
clearing rules; do not clear a multi-recipient item merely because Codex has
addressed its part.
